Notes on the attention economy

2025-11-26

Some short notes on how platforms optimize for engagement, and how that shapes our feeds and our sense of what matters.

Attention is a finite resource. Platforms treat it as something to be captured and held; people experience it as something that gets fragmented and exhausted.
