On culture wars and feeds

2025-11-26

Observations on how algorithmic distribution heightens conflict and erodes context. The arguments that survive are the ones that travel well in feeds, not necessarily the ones that are most true or helpful.

I’m interested in smaller, calmer online spaces where context isn’t always getting stripped away.
